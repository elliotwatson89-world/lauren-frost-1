Lauren Frost

Treatment Writer

Lauren has written winning treatments for brands including Mercedes, McDonald's, Prada, Kenzo, the FIFA Women's World Cup and Porsche.

Samples

Mercedes-Benz Campaign
A cinematic exploration of modern luxury through the lens of sustainable innovation. The treatment weaves together intimate character moments with sweeping automotive poetry, positioning the vehicle not as a product but as a partner in life's most meaningful journeys. Each frame is carefully constructed to balance technical precision with emotional resonance.
McDonald's Global Initiative
A celebration of everyday moments that become extraordinary through simple acts of connection. This treatment embraces authenticity and diversity, creating a tapestry of human experiences unified by shared joy. The approach is documentary-influenced yet stylistically bold, capturing the brand's evolution while honoring its universal appeal.
Prada Fashion Film
An atmospheric meditation on form, texture, and the architecture of desire. The treatment constructs a visual language that speaks to fashion as art, exploring the interplay between garment and wearer through precisely choreographed movements. Each sequence builds toward a crescendo of aesthetic philosophy made tangible.
FIFA Women's World Cup
A powerful narrative celebrating athleticism, determination, and the breaking of barriers. This treatment captures the electric energy of competition while highlighting the personal stories behind the players. The approach balances epic sporting moments with intimate portraits of dedication, creating an anthem for a new era in football.
Porsche Heritage Series
An ode to engineering excellence and the romance of the open road. The treatment honors Porsche's legendary history while firmly looking forward, blending archival sensibility with contemporary visual language. Each shot is crafted to emphasize both the machine's perfection and the visceral experience of driving it.
